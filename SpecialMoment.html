Hari Raya Memories

Hari Raya is a special time for us to celebrate with our loved ones. We gather with our family, wear beautiful traditional clothes, enjoy delicious food together, and visit relatives and friends. It is a joyful occasion filled with laughter, love, and unforgettable memories that bring us closer to one another.

Family Day Memories

Family Day is one of my favourite memories because everyone gathers to have fun and enjoy each other’s company. We play games, eat together, take photos, and laugh a lot. These precious moments make our relationship stronger and create memories that I will always treasure.
